# Omniflow Design Style 12: Pricing Desk

**Cocok untuk:** DealDesk, quotation, pricing, sales proposals, multi-company documents.

## Creative Thesis

Quotation adalah keputusan komersial yang dirakit. Desain menunjukkan proses dari brief mentah, struktur harga, koreksi manusia, sampai dokumen yang siap dikirim.

## Signature Device

**Redline Price:** satu nilai harga terus direvisi, diberi catatan, dihitung ulang, lalu dikunci sebagai grand total.

## Palette

- Proposal White `#F7F4EC`
- Executive Navy `#172A46`
- Red Pencil `#D84545`
- Brass `#B89545`
- Decision Blue `#2E63FF`
- Margin Gray `#8B9098`

## Typography

- Display: Libre Franklin Black
- Brand/body: Outfit
- Pricing/legal: IBM Plex Mono

## Material Grammar

- proposal pages;
- transparent tracing sheets;
- redline markup;
- calculator tape;
- signature block;
- page tabs;
- legal clause numbering.

## Scene Types

1. Client Brief Sheet
2. AI Draft Assembly
3. Line Item Calculator
4. Human Redline
5. Terms Composer
6. Company Issuer Plate
7. Quotation PDF Spread
8. Locked Total

## Narrative Sequence

`BRIEF → DRAFT → PRICE → REVIEW → CUSTOMIZE → NUMBER → SIGN → SEND`

## Avoid

- AI menulis quotation tanpa review.
- Generic invoice mockup.
- Semua slide seperti dokumen A4.
- Tanda tangan atau perusahaan nyata.
- Harga simulasi tanpa label.

## QA

- [ ] Human review terlihat eksplisit.
- [ ] Redline Price berubah minimal tiga kali.
- [ ] Terms dan exclusions dibedakan dari line item.
- [ ] Nomor quotation memiliki konteks.
- [ ] Closing menunjukkan dokumen final, bukan CTA button.
