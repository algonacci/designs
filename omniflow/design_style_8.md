# Omniflow Design Style 8: Cashflow Seismograph

**Cocok untuk:** Bank statement analytics, accounting, treasury, cashflow monitoring.

## Creative Thesis

Cashflow adalah gerakan, bukan saldo akhir. Desain membaca transaksi seperti seismograf yang merekam tekanan, pola berulang, dan kejadian tidak biasa.

## Signature Device

**Balance Horizon:** garis saldo berjalan kontinu, naik turun, melewati inflow, outflow, recurring event, anomaly, dan closing balance.

## Palette

- Statement Bone `#EEE9DD`
- Bank Blue `#1647A8`
- Carbon `#17191D`
- Inflow Mint `#5ED6A1`
- Outflow Red `#F04D4D`
- Alert Yellow `#F5C84C`

## Typography

- Display: Sora
- Brand/body: Outfit
- Transaction data: IBM Plex Mono

## Material Grammar

- bank-statement strips;
- seismograph line;
- debit/credit impulses;
- counterparty nodes;
- periodic wave;
- anomaly ring.

## Scene Types

1. Statement Intake
2. Debit Credit Split
3. Balance Horizon
4. Counterparty Orbit
5. Recurring Rhythm
6. Concentration Pressure
7. Anomaly Shock
8. Cashflow Reading

## Narrative Sequence

`PARSE → SEPARATE → TRACE → GROUP → REPEAT → CONCENTRATE → SIGNAL → READ`

## Avoid

- Menyebut anomaly sebagai fraud.
- Red/green tanpa label.
- Candlestick chart seperti trading app.
- Receipt styling.
- Saldo atau nama pihak nyata.

## QA

- [ ] Balance Horizon tersambung antar-slide.
- [ ] Debit dan kredit selalu memiliki label.
- [ ] Anomaly dijelaskan sebagai sinyal statistik.
- [ ] Unit IDR konsisten.
- [ ] Closing slide menjelaskan keputusan, bukan prediksi pasti.
