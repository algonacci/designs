# Omniflow Design Style 20: Field Dispatch

**Cocok untuk:** Helpdesk, field service, asset maintenance, telemarketing operations, mobile workforce.

## Creative Thesis

Pekerjaan lapangan adalah estafet tanggung jawab. Masalah harus diterima, diklasifikasikan, ditugaskan, dikerjakan, dibuktikan, dan ditutup.

## Signature Device

**Dispatch Patch:** patch berbentuk label tugas berpindah antar-slide dan berubah dari ticket, priority marker, assignee badge, route card, proof tag, SLA clock, dan closure seal.

## Palette

- Dispatch Orange `#F06A32`
- Utility Navy `#19334D`
- Field Sand `#E5D8C4`
- Signal Red `#D84949`
- Complete Green `#43936B`
- Work White `#F7F5EE`

## Typography

- Display: Barlow Condensed Black
- Brand/body: Outfit
- Task/status: IBM Plex Mono

## Material Grammar

- dispatch patches;
- work-order tags;
- SLA clocks;
- technician routes;
- priority chevrons;
- proof-of-work frames;
- closure seals;
- mobile status strips.

## Scene Types

1. Incoming Issue
2. Triage Board
3. Priority Patch
4. Assignment Relay
5. Field Route
6. Proof of Work
7. SLA Control
8. Closure Seal

## Narrative Sequence

`REPORT → TRIAGE → PRIORITIZE → ASSIGN → ARRIVE → PROVE → VERIFY → CLOSE`

## Avoid

- Customer support headset photo.
- Ticket cards memenuhi semua slide.
- Peta rute tanpa status pekerjaan.
- SLA sebagai countdown dekoratif.
- Checklist generik tanpa evidence.

## QA

- [ ] Dispatch Patch memiliki owner pada setiap tahap.
- [ ] Priority dan SLA tidak tercampur.
- [ ] Proof of work terlihat sebelum closure.
- [ ] Field data memakai skenario simulasi.
- [ ] Penutup menunjukkan resolved loop dan audit trace.
