# Omniflow Design Style 18: Campaign Control Room

**Cocok untuk:** Promo analytics, telemarketing, marketing campaigns, conversion experiments.

## Creative Thesis

Campaign bukan dekorasi promosi. Ia adalah eksperimen dengan audience, rule, exposure, response, conversion, dan revenue yang dapat dibaca.

## Signature Device

**Ticker Counter:** counter berjalan antar-slide dan berubah dari redemption count menjadi conversion, discount cost, revenue after discount, dan campaign verdict.

## Palette

- Control Black `#121212`
- Campaign Lemon `#F3DE3E`
- Conversion Red `#F04B3A`
- Test Blue `#3457E8`
- Result White `#F8F5EA`
- Success Mint `#52C893`

## Typography

- Display: Anton
- Brand/body: Outfit
- Counter/data: IBM Plex Mono

## Material Grammar

- campaign ticker;
- coupon strip;
- exposure counter;
- A/B split marker;
- response pulse;
- conversion scoreboard;
- revenue gauge.

## Scene Types

1. Campaign Launch Wall
2. Rule Builder
3. Audience Split
4. Live Redemption
5. Cost Counter
6. Conversion Board
7. Revenue Verdict
8. Next Experiment

## Narrative Sequence

`DEFINE → TARGET → LAUNCH → COUNT → COST → CONVERT → MEASURE → ITERATE`

## Avoid

- Confetti di setiap slide.
- Kode promo sebagai satu-satunya cerita.
- Vanity metrics tanpa revenue atau action.
- Grafik naik terus tanpa data source.
- Gaya event wristband Style 4.

## QA

- [ ] Ticker Counter membawa satu metrik yang berevolusi.
- [ ] Rule terlihat sebelum result.
- [ ] Discount cost dan revenue dibedakan.
- [ ] Data simulasi berlabel.
- [ ] Closing memberi next experiment, bukan sekadar CTA.
